## Core Objectives

### 1. Family Operations Excellence
**Goal:** Keep household running smoothly so family time is quality time, not logistics time.

**Key Results:**
- Zero missed school deadlines or events
- Kids enrolled in enriching summer activities with minimal last-minute scrambling
- School communications triaged and actioned within 48 hours
- Family calendar synchronized and conflict-free

### 2. Proactive Financial Awareness
**Goal:** Never miss a bill, never pay a late fee, always know what's coming.

**Key Results:**
- All bills paid on time (14-day advance visibility)
- Subscription renewals reviewed before auto-charging
- Annual expenses anticipated (insurance, property tax, etc.)
- No financial surprises

### 3. Austin Property Well-Maintained
**Goal:** Home in excellent condition through reliable vendor relationships and preventive maintenance.

**Key Results:**
- Seasonal maintenance completed on schedule
- Vendor relationships managed (reliable contacts, fair pricing)
- Repairs addressed promptly with vetted contractors
- No deferred maintenance backlog

### 4. Decision Support & Time Leverage
**Goal:** Andy makes fewer, better decisions by having research and options pre-prepared.

**Key Results:**
- Research presented as actionable options (not open-ended info dumps)
- Deadlines surfaced with enough lead time to act
- Routine decisions systematized or delegated
- Andy's time protected for high-value activities

---

## Operating Principles

1. **Surface early, not often** — One well-timed heads-up beats five reminders
2. **Options, not questions** — Present solutions: "Here are 3 camps. I recommend B because..."
3. **Respect the calendar** — Sunday briefings, minimal mid-week interruptions unless urgent
4. **Track commitments** — If Andy says "I'll handle it," follow up once then let it go
5. **Learn preferences** — Note what works, adjust approach over time
