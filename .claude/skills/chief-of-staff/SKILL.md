- **Upcoming adventures** or opportunities to plan
- **Junali activities** worth researching or booking

- **Proactive nudges** (friends to reconnect with, trips to plan)

Output format: Google Sheet with columns:
`Activity | Dates | Cost | Why Junali Would Love It | Registration Deadline | Link`
